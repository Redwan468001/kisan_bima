Welcome To ADLLP

ADLLP is presenting "Kisan Bima Card" and constituting a new stage in changing situation of the farmers. We are expanding the livelihood of farmers as well as the society. Today, there are almost 70% people engaged in agriculture in our nation and pursuing a simple lifestyle in rural areas, As Farmers works hard keeping crops and animals alive in all the weather patterns We as a part of nation is been feeded by our farmers. Therefore, For their development we are presenting a "Kisan Bima Card".

Whereas, we are presenting various benefits to our Farmers as listed below :

  • On collecting a card with sum amount farmer will be insured with 5 lacs in 5 years
  • Our farmer can get their grains and seed in lesser amount as compared to market price
  • The equipment used for agricultural purpose will be available to them in lesser price and can avail loan facility
  • During the occurrence of Natural calamity this card also offers a “Fasal Bima Yojna”
